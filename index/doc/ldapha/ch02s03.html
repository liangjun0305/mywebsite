3. PC节点准备

3.1. 设置主机名

master节点：

vi /etc/HOSTNAME
master

slave节点：

vi /etc/HOSTNAME
slave

3.2. 设置网络

master节点：

vi /etc/sysconfig/network/ifcfg-eth0
BOOTPROTO='static'
BROADCAST=''
ETHTOOL_OPTIONS=''
IPADDR='192.168.1.70/24'
MTU=''
NAME='82540EM Gigabit Ethernet Controller'
NETMASK=''
NETWORK=''
REMOTE_IPADDR=''
STARTMODE='auto'
USERCONTROL='no'
vi /etc/sysconfig/network/ifcfg-eth1
BOOTPROTO='static'
BROADCAST=''
ETHTOOL_OPTIONS=''
IPADDR='10.0.0.1/24'
MTU=''
NAME='82540EM Gigabit Ethernet Controller'
NETMASK=''
NETWORK=''
REMOTE_IPADDR=''
STARTMODE='auto'
USERCONTROL='no'

slave节点：

3.3. 设置主机名解析

master节点：

vi /etc/hosts
127.0.0.1       localhost
10.0.0.1       master
10.0.0.2       slave

slave节点：

vi /etc/hosts
127.0.0.1       localhost
10.0.0.1       master
10.0.0.2       slave
