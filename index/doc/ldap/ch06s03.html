3. 配置Courier imapd

vi /usr/local/courier-authlib/etc/authlib/authldaprc

LDAP_URI                ldaps://localhost
LDAP_PROTOCOL_VERSION   3
LDAP_BASEDN             dc=test.com
LDAP_BINDDN             cn=Manager, dc=test.com
LDAP_BINDPW             secret
LDAP_HOMEDIR            homeDirectory
LDAP_MAILROOT           /home/vmail
LDAP_MAILDIR            ./Maildir
LDAP_UID                uidNumber
LDAP_GID                gidNumber
